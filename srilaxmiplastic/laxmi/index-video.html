Our Journey & Expertise

Sri Laxmi Plastics was established in 1998 with a vision to revolutionize garment plastic hanger manufacturing. With over two decades of experience, we have become a trusted name in packaging solutions.

Research and development are at the core of our philosophy. We continuously invest in advanced technology to deliver high-quality products within competitive lead times. Our in-house tool room facilities, equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, ensure precision and consistency in every product.
